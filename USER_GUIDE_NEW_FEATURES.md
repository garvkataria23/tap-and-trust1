# 🎵 Music Albums & 📊 Diary Stats - User Guide

## 🎵 Music Albums Feature (NEW!)

### What Can You Do?
Create your own custom music albums to organize and manage your favorite songs!

### Getting Started
1. **Navigate to Music Page**
   - Click the Music button from your Dashboard
   - You'll see two tabs: "Music History" and "My Albums"

2. **Create a New Album**
   - Click the "New Album" button (blue gradient button)
   - Fill in the album details:
     - **Album Name**: Required - Give your album a catchy name
     - **Description**: Optional - Describe what's in your album
     - **Cover Image URL**: Optional - Add a cover image link
     - **Public/Private**: Toggle to make it visible to others
   - Click "Create Album"

3. **View Your Albums**
   - Albums display in a beautiful grid layout
   - Each card shows:
     - Album cover image (or music icon if no image)
     - Album name and description
     - Number of songs and total duration
     - Like count with heart icon
   - Click heart to like/unlike albums

4. **Manage Albums**
   - **Edit**: Hover over album and click pencil icon to update details
   - **Delete**: Hover over album and click trash icon to remove
   - **Share**: Share button available (coming soon)
   - **Like**: Click heart to like your favorite albums

5. **Add Songs to Albums** (Coming Soon)
   - Select songs from your music history
   - Add them to your albums
   - Create themed playlists

### Album Features
✅ Unlimited albums
✅ Custom cover images
✅ Album descriptions
✅ Public/private toggling
✅ Like functionality
✅ Duration tracking
✅ Song management

## 📊 Diary Statistics Feature (NEW!)

### What Can You Do?
Track your emotional journey with detailed analytics and mood insights!

### Getting Started
1. **Navigate to Diary Page**
   - Click the Diary button from your Dashboard
   - You'll see stats panel on the left, diary entries on the right

2. **View Your Statistics**
   - **Total Entries**: See how many diary entries you've written
   - **Average Words**: Track your typical entry length
   - **Current Streak**: See how many days in a row you've written
   - **Longest Streak**: View your personal best writing streak!

3. **Mood Distribution Chart**
   - Visual bar chart showing mood patterns
   - Percentage breakdown for each mood
   - Emoji indicators for each mood type
   - See which emotions dominate your writing

4. **Mood Types (8 Total)**
   - 😊 Happy
   - 😢 Sad
   - 🤩 Excited
   - 😰 Anxious
   - 😌 Calm
   - ✨ Inspired
   - 🙏 Grateful
   - 🤔 Reflective

5. **Filter Entries by Mood**
   - Click mood emoji buttons to filter entries
   - Multi-select for combined filters
   - Click "Clear Filters" to reset
   - See your emotional patterns in real-time

### Statistics You Can Track
✅ Total entries written
✅ Mood distribution breakdown
✅ Most frequent mood
✅ Average words per entry
✅ Current writing streak
✅ Longest writing streak
✅ Last entry date
✅ Mood-based entry filtering

### How to Use Mood Filtering
1. Look at the mood filter section
2. Click any mood emoji you want to focus on
3. Your diary entries will filter to show only those moods
4. Click multiple moods to see combined entries
5. Click "Clear Filters" to see all entries again

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### Albums Not Showing?
- Refresh the page
- Check your internet connection
- Make sure you're logged in
- Try creating a new album

### Stats Not Updating?
- Refresh the page
- Write a new diary entry
- Stats calculate automatically
- Check if entries have moods assigned

### Filter Not Working?
- Clear all filters and try again
- Refresh the page
- Make sure you have entries with those moods
- Check console for errors
